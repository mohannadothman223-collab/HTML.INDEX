Perfect Steak

This recipe shows how to cook a juicy steak with simple ingredients and easy-to-follow steps.

Ingredients

  • 1 ribeye steak (about 250g)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary

Method

  1. Remove the steak from the fridge and pat it dry with paper towels.
  2. Season both sides with salt and black pepper.
  3. Heat the olive oil in a frying pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Add the steak to the hot pan and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side.
  5. Add garlic and rosemary to the pan for extra flavour.
  6. Rest the steak on a plate for 5 minutes before slicing.
