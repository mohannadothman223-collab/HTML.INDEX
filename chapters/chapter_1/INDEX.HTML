Perfect Steak

This page explains how to cook a juicy, flavorful steak using simple ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 sirloin steak (about 250g)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 sprig rosemary

Method

  1. Take the steak out of the fridge and pat it dry with paper towels.
  2. Season both sides with salt and black pepper.
  3. Heat the olive oil in a frying pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Cook the steak for 3-4 minutes on each side for medium-rare.
  5. Add garlic and rosemary to the pan while cooking for extra flavour.
  6. Leave the steak to rest for 5 minutes before slicing and serving.
